# Health OS - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction

Health OS ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our health management platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information:** Date of birth, gender, health preferences
- **Contact Information:** Email, phone number (optional)

### Health Information
- **Medical Reports:** Uploaded health documents and reports
- **Health Metrics:** Extracted data from your reports (lab results, vitals, etc.)
- **Health History:** Timeline of your health data
- **User-Generated Content:** Notes, comments, and annotations

### Usage Information
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, pages viewed
- **Analytics:** How you interact with our platform
- **Cookies:** Session data, preferences, authentication

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- **Service Delivery:** Provide health report analysis and insights
- **AI Processing:** Extract and analyze health metrics from documents
- **Personalization:** Customize your dashboard and recommendations
- **Communication:** Send notifications about your health data
- **Support:** Respond to your questions and provide assistance

### Secondary Uses
- **Improvement:** Enhance our AI models and platform features
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
- **Compliance:** Meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Research:** Anonymized data for health research (opt-in only)

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT:
❌ Sell your personal or health information  
❌ Share data with advertisers  
❌ Use your data for marketing without consent  
❌ Share identifiable health data without permission  

### We MAY Share:
✅ **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly share reports  
✅ **Service Providers:** Trusted vendors who help operate our platform  
✅ **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect rights  
✅ **Anonymized Data:** For research with your opt-in consent  

### Service Providers
We work with:
- **Cloud Hosting:** AWS/GCP for secure data storage
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics (anonymized)
- **Email:** SendGrid for transactional emails
- **Payment:** Stripe for payment processing

All providers are HIPAA-compliant and bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

## Data Security

### Security Measures
- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Access Control:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
- **Audits:** Regular security audits and penetration testing
- **Backups:** Encrypted daily backups with disaster recovery

### HIPAA Compliance
We are HIPAA compliant and follow all required safeguards for Protected Health Information (PHI).

### GDPR Compliance
We comply with GDPR for users in the European Union, including:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Data:** Access all your personal and health data
- **Download Data:** Export your data in JSON or CSV format
- **Edit Data:** Update your profile and health information
- **Delete Data:** Request deletion of your account and all data

### Privacy Settings
- **Visibility:** Control who can see your data
- **Sharing:** Manage report sharing permissions
- **Notifications:** Choose what notifications you receive
- **Analytics:** Opt out of usage analytics
- **Research:** Opt in/out of anonymized research data

### Communication Preferences
- **Email:** Manage email notification preferences
- **Push:** Control browser push notifications
- **SMS:** Opt in/out of SMS notifications (if enabled)

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
- Data retained as long as your account is active
- You can delete individual reports anytime
- You can export your data anytime

### Deleted Accounts
- Account data deleted within 30 days
- Backups purged within 90 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance

### Legal Requirements
We may retain certain data longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., dispute resolution).

## Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies
- **Authentication:** Keep you logged in
- **Security:** Prevent fraud and abuse
- **Preferences:** Remember your settings

### Analytics Cookies
- **Usage:** Understand how you use our platform
- **Performance:** Identify and fix issues
- **Optimization:** Improve user experience

You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy

Health OS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

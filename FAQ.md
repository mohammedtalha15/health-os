# Health OS - FAQ

## General Questions

### What is Health OS?

Health OS is a personal health clarity engine that helps you upload, analyze, and understand your medical reports. Using AI-powered technology, we extract key health metrics and provide insights to help you track your health journey.

### Is Health OS free to use?

We offer a free tier with basic features. Premium plans are available for advanced analytics, unlimited uploads, and additional features. See our [Pricing](https://healthos.com/pricing) page for details.

### Is my health data secure?

Yes! We take security very seriously:
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We comply with HIPAA and GDPR regulations
- Your data is never shared without your explicit consent
- We use industry-standard security practices
- Regular security audits are performed

See our [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md) for more details.

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?

1. Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile
5. Start uploading reports!

### What types of reports can I upload?

We support:
- Lab test results (blood work, urine tests, etc.)
- Prescription documents
- Discharge summaries
- Imaging reports (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans)
- Doctor's notes
- Vaccination records

Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX (up to 10MB per file)

### How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI:
1. Extracts text from your uploaded documents using OCR
2. Identifies health metrics and values
3. Compares values against reference ranges
4. Generates easy-to-understand explanations
5. Tracks trends over time

The process typically takes 30-60 seconds per document.

## Using Health OS

### How do I upload a report?

1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click "Upload Report"
3. Select your file or drag and drop
4. Add a title and select the report type
5. Click "Upload"
6. Wait for AI processing to complete

### Can I edit or delete reports?

Yes! You can:
- Edit report titles and types
- Delete reports you no longer need
- Re-upload if processing failed
- Download original files anytime

### How do I track metrics over time?

1. Upload multiple reports with the same metrics
2. Go to the Timeline view
3. Select the metric you want to track
4. View trends, charts, and insights

### Can I share my reports with doctors?

Yes! You can:
- Generate shareable links (with expiration)
- Export reports as PDF
- Email reports directly
- Print reports for appointments

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

We support:
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

Mobile browsers are also fully supported.

### Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Health OS is a web application optimized for mobile browsers. Native iOS and Android apps are planned for Q2 2024. See our [Roadmap](./ROADMAP.md).

### Can I use Health OS offline?

Basic viewing of previously loaded data works offline. Uploading and AI analysis require an internet connection. Full offline support is planned for the mobile apps.

### How do I export my data?

1. Go to Settings
2. Click "Export Data"
3. Select format (JSON, CSV, or PDF)
4. Choose date range
5. Download your data

You own your data and can export it anytime!

## Privacy & Security

### Who can see my health data?

Only you can see your health data unless you explicitly share it. We never:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for purposes other than providing our service

### How long is my data stored?

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. You can:
- Delete individual reports anytime
- Delete your entire account and all data
- Request data export before deletion

### Is Health OS HIPAA compliant?

Yes! We are HIPAA compliant and follow all required security and privacy practices for handling protected health information (PHI).

### Do you use my data to train AI?

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve our AI models. Individual health data is never used without explicit consent. You can opt out in Settings.

## Troubleshooting

### My report upload failed. What should I do?

Try these steps:
1. Check file size (max 10MB)
2. Verify file format (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX)
3. Ensure stable internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issue persists

### The AI didn't extract my metrics correctly. Why?

This can happen if:
- The document quality is poor
- The format is unusual
- Handwritten notes are present
- The document is in a non-English language

You can manually add metrics or contact support for help.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with new password

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to Settings → Account
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Enter password
5. Account and all data will be permanently deleted

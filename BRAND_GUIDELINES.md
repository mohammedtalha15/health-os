# Health OS - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Mission Statement
"Empowering individuals to understand and take control of their health through AI-powered clarity."

### Vision
"A world where everyone has instant access to clear, actionable insights about their health."

### Values
- **Clarity** - Making complex health data simple
- **Privacy** - Your health data belongs to you
- **Accuracy** - Reliable AI-powered analysis
- **Accessibility** - Health insights for everyone
- **Innovation** - Continuous improvement

## Logo

### Primary Logo
- **Format**: SVG (vector)
- **Colors**: Full color on light backgrounds
- **Minimum size**: 120px width
- **Clear space**: Minimum 20px on all sides

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Color** - Primary use
2. **White** - On dark backgrounds
3. **Black** - On light backgrounds
4. **Monochrome** - When color isn't available

### Logo Don'ts
❌ Don't stretch or distort  
❌ Don't change colors  
❌ Don't add effects (shadows, gradients)  
❌ Don't rotate  
❌ Don't place on busy backgrounds

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice

**Friendly** - Approachable and warm  
**Clear** - Simple and understandable  
**Confident** - Trustworthy and reliable  
**Supportive** - Helpful and encouraging  

### Writing Guidelines

**Do:**
- Use simple, clear language
- Be concise and direct
- Use active voice
- Explain medical terms
- Be encouraging

**Don't:**
- Use jargon without explanation
- Be overly technical
- Use fear-based messaging
- Make medical diagnoses
- Guarantee outcomes

### Example Copy

**Good:**
"Your cholesterol level is within the healthy range. Keep up the good work!"

**Bad:**
"LDL-C value 95 mg/dL falls within reference parameters."

## Animation

### Principles

- **Purposeful** - Enhance understanding
- **Subtle** - Don't distract
- **Fast** - 150-300ms
- **Smooth** - Ease-in-out curves

### Common Animations

- **Fade in**: 200ms
- **Slide in**: 300ms
- **Scale**: 150ms
- **Hover**: 100ms

## Accessibility

### Contrast Ratios

- **Normal text**: 4.5:1 minimum
- **Large text**: 3:1 minimum
- **UI components**: 3:1 minimum

### Focus States

- Visible focus indicators
- 2px outline
- Primary Blue color
- 2px offset

### Touch Targets

- Minimum size: 44x44px
- Adequate spacing
- Clear hit areas
